Hey, nice to have you here. My name is Maricel.

I was born in Rio Gallegos, a small city in the deepest of Patagonia, almost at the verge of the American Continent.

I lived in Mar del Plata, Buenos Aires, Malaga, Barcelona, London, Huelva, Cambridge, Mexico City, Tepoztlán, Worthing and Brighton.

I travelled around Morocco, hitchhiked throughout Europe and got lost in Uruguay.

I have a Media Award in Photojournalism from the London College of Communication, and surprisingly I was finalist at the AOP Open Awards in 2007.

The year 2012 I finished my degree in Film Studies and History and I went with a huge project I was part of, to the Olympic Games Cultural Programme.

Ho no….. I haven’t run any marathon just yet.

I had my first solo exhibition on the theme City Lights in Mexico City. I fell in love with Mexico, I hope to come back some day.

In 2014, I went to the Ojo de Pez photo meeting Barcelona.

Eventually, I moved back to Great Britain where I work, eat and live.

I speak English, Spanish and Italian and when I’m angry or anxious I speak them all at the same time. Which makes a unique language.

The year 2016, I was at the Brighton Photo Fringe with my Trash. I mean the name of the project was “Trash”.

Then last year I began to fall in love with web design. The process of creating something out of nothing is something that I really enjoy.

So I taught myself how to code, and now I make websites on a freelance basis. Of course I'm learning every single day.

Is remote working the way to go?

I think it has the potential of becoming a path for making a living. Future generation will laught at our 9.oo to 5.00 soul sucking corporate jobs.

With half of our jobs being aautomated and the other half overtaken by robots, we're kind of forced to look into our abilities and passions;

We must find work that really makes us happy.

Things are changing super fast, and the way our parents used to live is increasingly obsolete.

We must find ways to do things that are meaningful to us. This is not bad, is not a revolution either.

I'd say is an evolution and only the most adaptable human beings will be able to thrive.

So here I am making websites to hep you thrive and make the most of the the world wide web.
